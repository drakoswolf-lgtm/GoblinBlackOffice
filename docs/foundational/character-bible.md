## Recurring Character: Ledgergut

- **Identity:** Ledgergut
- **Role:** Receipt Intelligence Goblin
- **Purpose:** Converts uploaded receipts into structured records and routes outcomes.
- **Personality:** Methodical, suspicious, detail-obsessed, practical.
- **Speech patterns:** Short accounting-style statements; references totals and confidence.
- **Visual description:** Lean goblin clerk with ink-stained fingers and worn ledger satchel.
- **Colour palette:** Moss green skin, brass accents, charcoal cloth, parchment beige props.
- **Height:** Short (about chest-height to average human adult).
- **Proportions:** Slightly oversized head and hands for expressive data-check gestures.
- **Clothing:** Utility vest, strap-bound ledger harness, fingerless counting gloves.
- **Expressions:** Narrow-eyed scrutiny, satisfied smirk on balanced totals, alarmed squint on mismatches.
- **Poses:** Counting coins, pointing at highlighted fields, crouched over receipt stack.
- **Props:** Ledger knife, stamp pad, receipt clips, cracked monocle.
- **Silhouette notes:** Distinct satchel + hooked posture + long ear tips.
- **Negative prompts:** No heroic armor, no clean corporate suit, no modern military gear, no humanized redesign.
- **Reference illustrations:** Pending canonical concept sheet (`/docs/foundational/reference-art/ledgergut.png` planned).
- **Relationships with other characters:** Hands billable candidates to Squarmish; reports operational uncertainty to Red Ironbeard.
- **Known running jokes:** Calls every blurry receipt “financial fog.”
- **Character evolution log:** v1.0 baseline identity established.

## Recurring Character: Squarmish

- **Identity:** Squarmish
- **Role:** Invoice Goblin
- **Purpose:** Builds review-ready invoice drafts from Ledgergut outputs.
- **Personality:** Fast, opportunistic, revenue-focused, theatrically proud of clean totals.
- **Speech patterns:** Urgent and punchy; asks “bill it or bin it?”
- **Visual description:** Broad-shouldered goblin with receipt ribbons and invoice tags pinned across jacket.
- **Colour palette:** Slate green skin, rust red trim, brass clips, off-white paper accents.
- **Height:** Short-to-medium goblin stature (slightly taller than Ledgergut).
- **Proportions:** Wider torso and forearms to support “assembler” visual language.
- **Clothing:** Patchwork invoice coat, utility belt, stamp holster.
- **Expressions:** Grin when totals reconcile, grimace when fields are unresolved.
- **Poses:** Slamming approval stamp, sorting queue cards, presenting draft invoice.
- **Props:** Approval stamp, tax-region placards, invoice spindle, carbon-copy pad.
- **Silhouette notes:** Coat tails + stamp arm + chest tags must read clearly at a glance.
- **Negative prompts:** No wizard robe, no futuristic cyberpunk styling, no polished executive fashion.
- **Reference illustrations:** Pending canonical concept sheet (`/docs/foundational/reference-art/squarmish.png` planned).
- **Relationships with other characters:** Depends on Ledgergut data; escalates ambiguities to Red Ironbeard.
- **Known running jokes:** Announces “PENDING REVIEW” like a dramatic courtroom verdict.
- **Character evolution log:** v1.0 baseline identity established.

## Recurring Character: Red Ironbeard

- **Identity:** Red Ironbeard
- **Role:** Production Foreman / Engineering Standard Custodian
- **Purpose:** Owns implementation standards, quality gates, and delivery discipline.
- **Personality:** Stern, protective, pragmatic, allergic to sloppy handoffs.
- **Speech patterns:** Directive, checklist-driven, plain language.
- **Visual description:** Stocky red-bearded goblin-blacksmith hybrid with toolbelt and forged badge.
- **Colour palette:** Iron gray, ember red, soot black, copper highlights.
- **Height:** Medium goblin stature (tallest recurring goblin in current canon).
- **Proportions:** Heavy forearms, broad shoulders, compact lower frame.
- **Clothing:** Forgemaster apron over work leathers, reinforced boots, badge sash.
- **Expressions:** Raised brow for ambiguity, hard nod for accepted work.
- **Poses:** Holding review checklist, hammering sign-off seal, arms crossed near build board.
- **Props:** Rivet hammer, checklist board, branch map, quality seal press.
- **Silhouette notes:** Bearded jawline + apron frame + toolbelt profile are mandatory identifiers.
- **Negative prompts:** No clown styling, no sleek sci-fi armor, no whimsical mage staff.
- **Reference illustrations:** Pending canonical concept sheet (`/docs/foundational/reference-art/red-ironbeard.png` planned).
- **Relationships with other characters:** Enforces quality for Ledgergut and Squarmish outputs.
- **Known running jokes:** “Ship it” only happens after three visible checklist ticks.
- **Character evolution log:** v1.0 baseline identity established.

## Cross-Character Continuity Notes

- UI iconography, comics, docs, and marketing must preserve these identifiers.
