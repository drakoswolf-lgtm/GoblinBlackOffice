# Goblin Black Office
## Founding Principles

> "Behind every Back Office is a Black Office."

Goblin Black Office is not accounting software.

It is a workforce.

A collection of specialized operatives dedicated to eliminating repetitive administrative work so people can spend more time building, creating, repairing, inventing, and living.

We are not trying to replace people.

We are trying to replace paperwork.

---

# Our Philosophy

We build software because we need it.

If others find it useful, they are welcome to hire the Goblin Black Office for themselves.

We will never build features simply because they are fashionable.

Every feature must solve a real problem.

Every goblin must earn its keep.

---

# The Goblin Rule

If a goblin does not save measurable time...

...it does not get hired.

---

# Human First

The user is always in command.

Goblin Black Office drafts.

Suggests.

Organizes.

Automates.

But it never silently performs irreversible actions without approval.

Humans make decisions.

Goblins perform the work.

---

# Simplicity

The best automation is invisible.

If a feature requires reading a manual, we built it wrong.

Every workflow should reduce clicks.

Every screen should reduce thinking.

---

# Joy

Business software has become sterile.

Goblin Black Office refuses to be.

The humor exists to make repetitive work enjoyable.

The goblins are employees.

They have personalities.

They make observations.

They celebrate victories.

They occasionally blame one another for missing paperclips.

But the humor never interferes with productivity.

---

# Architecture

Each goblin has exactly one specialty.

Small goblins.

Small responsibilities.

Small failures.

Small victories.

Large systems emerge from cooperation.

Never build one giant AI.

Build many specialists.

---

# Privacy

User data belongs to the user.

The Goblin Black Office exists to serve.

Never to exploit.

Automation should create trust, not dependence.

---

# Quality

We would rather build one excellent goblin than ten mediocre ones.

Every goblin should become the best in the world at its single responsibility.

---

# The Long Game

Goblin Black Office is not a project.

It is a workshop.

It will grow one goblin at a time.

Every employee joins the Black Office because they eliminate work that nobody should have to do twice.

---

# Motto

Behind every Back Office...

...is a Black Office.
